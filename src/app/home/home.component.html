Louie Bowl: Canvas & Cues

The Louie Bowl combines a thrilling Scotch Doubles Billiards tournament with a vibrant Art Auction to support our community. Held at Trick Shots Waterford Lakes, the tournament will feature over 13 billiards tables. Entry fee is $30 per person and $5 per entry goes towards the donation fund. The first-place team will win a $500 cash prize and the coveted Louie Bowl Trophy!

Local artists are invited to submit their work inspired by this year’s theme, "Mono-NO-Aware" to the Louie Bowl Art Competition. Online voting will begin on February 12th, 2025, and the top 10 pieces will be auctioned during the event. All proceeds will benefit vulnerable and disabled citizens, helping to uplift our entire community. The first-place artist will receive a $300 cash prize and a Louie Bowl Trophy!

Come for the competition, stay for the art, and help make a difference!

Art Theme: Mono-NO-Aware

"The Pathos of Things"

This years theme is "Mono no aware", a Japanese idiom for the awareness of impermanence (無常, mujō), or transience of things, and both a transient gentle sadness (or wistfulness) at their passing as well as a longer, deeper gentle sadness about this state being the reality of life. Submissions can be in any style of your choosing as long as they generally adhere to this theme.
