Art Theme: Mono-no-aware

"The Pathos of Things"

This years theme is "Mono no aware", a Japanese idiom for the awareness of impermanence, or transience of things. The artist should reflect on the greater concept of life and may express through their work the beauty, sadness, or greater emotions that arise from this reflection. Submissions can be in any style of your choosing.

Commemoration

In Loving Memory of Luis Gabriel Gonzalez

Luis Gabriel Gonzalez, a beloved individual whose kindness, dedication, and compassion touched countless lives. Luis was known for his intense love of life and his deep care for others. His passion for living fully and his genuine compassion made a lasting impact on everyone he encountered. As we remember him today, his memory serves as a pillar of hope, a reminder that even in the face of adversity, we can strive for a better future and offer support to those in need. His example encourages us to live with empathy, to uplift one another, and to work together toward a brighter tomorrow.
